Concept

You are a Pokemon's fan and collect cards but want to find an alternative that will not empty your wallet?
You are in the right place! Make place for the PokeCards, unofficial cards to collect from the Pokemon Café's universe!

Find the same joy by discovering different rarity cards after opening boosters:
B for the commons, A for the rare and S for the super rare!

How do we recognize them? The B rank PokeCards have a green outline, A rank have a blue outline and S rank have a purple one!
