Frequently Asked Questions

How can I create an account?

▼

To create an account, click on the "Register" button at the top of the page. Fill in the form with your information and voilà!

Do I have to pay to access content?

▼

Absolutely not! This project is made to be accessible to everyone, I have no intention of making it paid.

What is a PokeCard?

▼

The PokeCards are unofficial collectible cards inspired by the universe of Pokemon Café Remix. They offer an affordable alternative to the official Pokemon cards while retaining the pleasure of the collection.

What are the different ranks of rarity?

▼

There are 3 ranks of rarity: rank B (common) with green border, rank A (rare) with blue border, and rank S (super rare) with purple border.

How can I get PokeCards?

▼

PokeCards are available as boosters, like real Pokémon cards. Each opening offers you surprises with different rarities!

Are PokeCards official Pokémon cards?

▼

No, PokeCards are unofficial fan creations. All images belong to The Pokemon Company and are used for personal collection purposes.

How to see all the available collection?

▼

You can view the entire collection by visiting the "Collection" page accessible from the navigation menu.
